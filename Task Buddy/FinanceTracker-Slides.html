Slide 1  ·  Project Description

What Is SpendEasy?

SpendEasy is a personal finance tracker built around one simple idea: you can't improve what you can't see. Instead of drowning users in complex graphs and synced bank accounts, SpendEasy lets people manually log what they spend, see the total at a glance, and spot patterns — all in a clean, distraction-free experience.
👁️

Instant Spending Overview

See your total outflow the moment you open the app. No hunting through menus — just clarity, right away.

🔒

Secure Personal Profiles

Each user has their own private account. Your financial data stays yours — protected and personal.

🗂️

Category-Based Tracking

Organize spending by merchant and type. Know exactly where your money goes — groceries, subscriptions, dining, and more.

🌙

Designed for Real Life

Dark mode for late-night record-keeping. Works beautifully on desktops and tablets — wherever life takes you.

Slide 2  ·  User Personas

Who Is This Built For?

👩‍💼
Sarah, 24
"The Budgeting Beginner"
Who she is Recent graduate, just started her first job. New to managing her own income.
Her struggle Reaches the end of every month shocked at how fast her money disappeared — with no idea where it went.
Her frustration Banking apps feel complicated and overwhelming. She just wants something simple.
Her goal Build a daily tracking habit so she can save enough for her first car.
SpendEasy gives her a fresh start — no complexity, no jargon.
👨‍🎨
David, 35
"The Conscious Consumer"
Who he is Freelance designer juggling multiple clients. No fixed salary — every expense matters.
His struggle Needs to separate business vs. personal spending, but existing tools slow him down.
His frustration Doesn't want to "sync" his bank every time he buys a coffee or pays for a tool subscription.
His goal Stay lean, control his monthly runway, and maintain financial freedom.
SpendEasy is fast, intentional, and always ready — like him.
Two very different people. One shared need: to understand where their money goes.

Slide 4  ·  The Impact

What Does SpendEasy
Change for People?

💡No more "where did it all go?"
For the first time, users see a clear picture of their spending — instantly, without digging through bank statements or complex dashboards.
🧠Builds better money habits
Manually entering each transaction makes spending feel intentional. Users think twice before spending — and that awareness is powerful.
🎯Goals become reachable
Whether it's saving for a car or cutting unnecessary subscriptions, SpendEasy turns vague goals into clear, trackable progress.
🚀Designed responsibly — for everyone
From a 24-year-old beginner to a 35-year-old freelancer — SpendEasy was built around real people, real frustrations, and real daily life.
